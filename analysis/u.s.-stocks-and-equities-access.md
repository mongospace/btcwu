# U.S. Equity Gateway

## Market Snapshot

The U.S. equities universe is the deepest pool of listed capital worldwide, exceeding $45T in market cap. BTCWU connects investors to this ecosystem with direct market access, transparent pricing, and workflows tailored to both everyday traders and institutional desks.

## Coverage Footprint

### Primary Venues

* **NYSE** for blue-chip and legacy listings.
* **NASDAQ** for growth, tech, and innovation leaders.
* **NYSE American (AMEX)** specializing in ETFs and small caps.
* **OTC Markets** enabling exposure to ADRs, pink sheets, and emerging issuers.
* **Regional Exchanges & ATSs** for localized liquidity.
* **Institutional Dark Pools** for discreet block execution.
