### Core Trading Engine

The heart of the Emcex platform is our advanced trading engine, designed to handle millions of transactions per second with sub-millisecond latency. Built on a microservices architecture, our trading engine provides:

* **Scalable Infrastructure**: Cloud-native architecture that scales automatically
* **Fault Tolerance**: Built-in redundancy and failover capabilities
* **Real-Time Processing**: Real-time market data processing and analysis
* **Multi-Asset Support**: Unified engine supporting all asset classes

* **Risk Management**: Integrated risk management and position monitoring
