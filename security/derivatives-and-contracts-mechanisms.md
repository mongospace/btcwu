# Derivatives and Contract Mechanisms

## Market Context

Derivatives remain the backbone of institutional hedging and speculation, with global notional exposure surpassing $600T. BTCWU delivers a unified venue covering futures, options, swaps, and structured products—supported by precision risk tooling and transparent governance.

## Instrument Spectrum

### Futures & Perpetuals
- Commodities: metals, energy, agri, and carbon.
- Financial: rates, FX, equity indices, and volatility benchmarks.
- Digital Assets: major tokens, baskets, and emerging sector indices.
- Custom deliverables for corporate hedging and structured desks.

### Options
- Plain vanilla on equities, FX, rates, commodities, and crypto pairs.
- Index overlays, protective collars, and long-vol strategies.
- Exotic structures (barrier, digital, Asian) with lifecycle automation.
- Flex options enabling bespoke expiries, strikes, and settlement styles.

### Swaps & Forwards
- Interest rate, cross-currency, and basis swaps with CSA support.
- Credit default swaps and total return swaps for institutional hedging.
- Tokenized asset swaps integrating on/off-chain collateral.

### Structured Notes & Certificates
- Principal-protected and yield-enhanced instruments.
- Thematic baskets tied to ESG, DeFi, or sector exposures.
- OTC contracts assembled through workflow-driven negotiation tools.

## Contract Design

### Specification Standards
- Standardized lots, expiries, and tick sizes aligned to market conventions.
- Cash or physical settlement choices with automated allocation workflows.
- Price bands, circuit breakers, and volatility interruption protocols.
- Initial/maintenance margin schedules with dynamic add-ons.
- Position and exposure limits tuned by client tier and regulatory jurisdiction.

### Pricing & Valuation
- Mark-to-market cycles with intraday variation margin.
- Volatility surfaces, skew/smile modeling, and live recalibration.
- Yield curve bootstrapping, credit spread analytics, and liquidity adjustments.
- Model risk governance with independent validation and backtesting.

## Risk Controls

### Market Greeks
- Real-time delta, gamma, vega, theta, rho, vanna, vomma, and charm analytics.
- Scenario engines modeling cross-gamma and basis risk.

### Credit & Counterparty
- Counterparty exposure dashboards, wrong-way risk detection, CVA/DVA.
- Collateral optimization, margin calls, and dispute resolution workflows.
- Legal agreements (ISDA, GMRA, CSA) digitally managed with e-signatures.

### Market Stress & Liquidity
- VaR, expected shortfall, Monte Carlo, historical simulation.
- Stress tests for rate shocks, volatility spikes, contagion events.
- Liquidity heatmaps, order book depth, and impact cost estimators.

## Execution Layer

### Order Mechanics
- Market, limit, stop, stop-limit, trailing, iceberg, and bulk orders.
- Time-in-force options (GTC, IOC, FOK, GTD) and conditional chains.
- Algorithmic suite: TWAP, VWAP, POV, implementation shortfall, opportunistic.

### Market Access
- Smart order routing across exchanges, dark pools, and OTC venues.
- Co-location, cross-connects, and low-latency gateways.
- Market-making APIs enabling streaming quotes and auto-hedging.

## Clearing & Settlement

### Central Clearing
- CCP partnerships with cross-margining and portfolio offsets.
- Initial/variation margin automation, collateral haircuts, and stress add-ons.
- Default management groups, auction protocols, and default fund layers.

### Bilateral & OTC
- Distributed ledger for trade capture, confirmation, and lifecycle events.
- Smart contracts handling coupon payments, resets, and corporate actions.
- Settlement workflows supporting T+0 to T+2, cash or physical delivery.

## Regulation & Compliance

- Adherence to MiFID II, EMIR, Dodd-Frank, CFTC, SEC, FCA, MAS, ASIC, IOSCO.
- Trade repository reporting (EMIR, CFTC Part 45/43) with straight-through processing.
- Position limits, large trader reporting, and market conduct surveillance.
- Know-your-customer, suitability checks, and best-execution policies.

## Technology Backbone

- Ultra-low latency matching engine with deterministic performance.
- Real-time risk engine co-located with order flow for pre-trade checks.
- FIX/REST/WebSocket connectivity, collocation, and cross-venue distribution.
- Market data platform delivering tick-level feeds, analytics, and replay.

### Professional & Retail
