# Derivatives and Contracts Mechanisms

## Derivatives Market Overview

The global derivatives market represents one of the largest and most sophisticated financial markets, with a notional value exceeding $600 trillion. Emcex provides comprehensive access to derivative instruments, offering advanced risk management tools, sophisticated pricing models, and institutional-grade execution capabilities.

## Types of Derivatives

### Futures Contracts
- **Commodity Futures**: Agricultural, energy, and metal futures
- **Financial Futures**: Interest rate, currency, and stock index futures
- **Cryptocurrency Futures**: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoin futures
- **Single Stock Futures**: Individual stock futures contracts
- **Volatility Futures**: VIX and other volatility index futures
- **Weather Futures**: Weather-based derivative contracts
- **Environmental Futures**: Carbon credit and environmental futures

### Options Contracts
- **Stock Options**: Individual stock options with various strike prices
- **Index Options**: Broad market index options (S&P 500, NASDAQ)
- **Currency Options**: Foreign exchange options
- **Commodity Options**: Options on commodity futures
- **Interest Rate Options**: Bond and interest rate options
- **Volatility Options**: Options on volatility indices
- **Exotic Options**: Barrier, Asian, and other exotic option structures

### Swaps
- **Interest Rate Swaps**: Fixed for floating rate swaps
- **Currency Swaps**: Cross-currency interest rate swaps
- **Credit Default Swaps**: Credit risk transfer instruments
- **Commodity Swaps**: Commodity price risk management
- **Equity Swaps**: Equity return swaps
- **Volatility Swaps**: Volatility exposure swaps
- **Total Return Swaps**: Total return exposure swaps

### Structured Products
- **Convertible Bonds**: Equity-linked debt instruments
- **Warrants**: Equity-linked derivative securities
- **Certificates**: Structured investment certificates
- **Notes**: Principal-protected and enhanced return notes
- **ETFs**: Exchange-traded funds with derivative exposure
- **ETNs**: Exchange-traded notes with derivative components
- **Structured Deposits**: Bank deposit products with derivative features

## Contract Specifications

### Standardization
- **Contract Sizes**: Standardized contract sizes for each instrument
- **Expiration Dates**: Regular expiration cycles (monthly, quarterly)
- **Settlement Methods**: Cash settlement vs. physical delivery
- **Trading Hours**: Specific trading hours for each contract
- **Price Limits**: Daily price movement limits and circuit breakers
- **Margin Requirements**: Initial and maintenance margin requirements
- **Position Limits**: Maximum position size limits per trader

### Pricing Mechanisms
- **Mark-to-Market**: Daily mark-to-market pricing
- **Fair Value Models**: Sophisticated pricing models for complex instruments
- **Volatility Surfaces**: Multi-dimensional volatility modeling
- **Interest Rate Curves**: Yield curve construction and modeling
- **Credit Spreads**: Credit risk premium calculations
- **Liquidity Adjustments**: Liquidity premium adjustments
- **Model Risk Management**: Model validation and risk management

## Risk Management

### Position Risk
- **Delta Risk**: Price sensitivity to underlying asset movements
- **Gamma Risk**: Rate of change of delta with underlying price
- **Theta Risk**: Time decay risk for options positions
- **Vega Risk**: Volatility sensitivity risk
- **Rho Risk**: Interest rate sensitivity risk
- **Cross-Gamma Risk**: Cross-asset correlation risk
- **Basis Risk**: Basis risk between cash and derivative markets

### Credit Risk
- **Counterparty Risk**: Risk of counterparty default
- **Settlement Risk**: Risk of settlement failure
- **Replacement Cost**: Cost of replacing defaulted positions
- **Credit Limits**: Counterparty credit exposure limits
- **Collateral Management**: Collateral posting and management
- **Netting Agreements**: Bilateral and multilateral netting
- **Credit Valuation Adjustment**: CVA calculation and management

### Market Risk
- **Value at Risk (VaR)**: Statistical risk measurement
- **Expected Shortfall**: Tail risk measurement
- **Stress Testing**: Scenario-based risk analysis
- **Monte Carlo Simulation**: Probabilistic risk modeling
- **Historical Simulation**: Historical scenario analysis
- **Factor Models**: Multi-factor risk models
- **Correlation Analysis**: Cross-asset correlation monitoring

- **Market Orders**: Immediate execution at best available price
- **Limit Orders**: Execution at specified price or better

- **TWAP**: Time-weighted average price execution

- **Adaptive Algorithms**: Market condition adaptive execution
- **Dark Pool Access**: Access to dark liquidity pools
- **Cross-Network Routing**: Multi-venue execution routing
