# Analytics and Automation Suite

## Intelligence Platform Overview

BTCWU equips traders with an integrated analytics stack that fuses real-time data, machine learning diagnostics, and highly visual workspaces. Every module is crafted to surface signal, streamline execution, and translate complex datasets into clear action.

## Market Data Fabric

### Live Market Intelligence
- **Streaming Quotes** from tier-one venues with depth-of-book and auction visibility.
- **Order Flow Heatmaps** exposing liquidity pockets and sweep activity.
- **Volatility Dashboards** tracking implied, realized, and cross-asset correlations.
- **Sentiment Gauges** blending news, social, and on-chain indicators.
- **Event Pulse** with synchronized macro calendars and instant impact scoring.

### Technical Studio
- **Chart Engine** featuring 100+ studies, multi-chart layouts, and scripting.
- **Pattern Radar** for candlestick, harmonic, Elliott, and custom detections.
- **Volume Profile & VWAP** layers with intraday and positional context.
- **Automated Trendlines** and regime detection overlays.

### Fundamental Lens
- **Financial Statement Explorer** with normalized ratios and peer benchmarking.
- **Earnings Workbench** including surprise tracking and scenario modeling.
- **Valuation Matrix** covering multiples, discounted cash flow, and relative comps.
- **Capital Structure Monitor** surfacing leverage, liquidity, and covenant flags.

## Portfolio Command Center

### Performance & Risk
- **Return Analytics** with absolute, risk-adjusted, and factor-based metrics.
- **Attribution Engine** decomposing contribution by asset, sector, and driver.
- **Risk Stack** delivering VaR, CVaR, drawdowns, beta, and stress narratives.
- **Correlation Map** overlaying cross-asset and currency exposures.

### Allocation Mechanics
- **Target vs. Actual Dashboards** highlighting drift and rebalance triggers.
- **Diversification Scores** and concentration alerts.
- **Risk Budgeting** tools assigning limits per asset, strategy, or desk.
- **Factor Exposure Analyzer** with style tilts and geographic overlays.

## Execution Intelligence

### Trade Analytics
- **Execution Quality Reports** benchmarking slippage, fill rates, and routing.
- **Market Impact Models** simulating footprint before large orders.
- **Cost Attribution** spanning commissions, spreads, borrow, and funding.
- **Timing Analysis** comparing intraday vs. session performance.

### Strategy Laboratory
- **Backtesting Grid** with tick-to-daily granularity and walk-forward testing.
- **Paper Trading Sandbox** synced with live data for rehearsal.
- **Optimization Engine** for parameter sweeps, scenario testing, and Monte Carlo.
- **Playbook Library** capturing best-practice tactics and shared strategies.

### Microstructure Insights
- **Liquidity Spectrum** showing depth, spreads, and adverse selection probabilities.
- **Order Book Replay** for forensic analysis and compliance audit.
- **Latency Metrics** across venues and routing paths.
- **Market Quality Scores** aiding venue selection per symbol.

## AI & Machine Learning Stack

### Predictive Modules
- **Price Forecasting** with ensemble models and confidence bands.
- **Volatility Outlooks** combining GARCH, deep learning, and event triggers.
- **Trend Classification** indicating breakout, range, or reversal regimes.
- **Sentiment Parsing** via NLP on news, filings, and social chatter.
- **Risk Early Warning** spotting anomaly clusters and liquidity crunch signals.

### Algorithmic Infrastructure
- **Strategy Builder** supporting code, drag-and-drop, and notebook workflows.
- **Execution Algorithms** including POV, TWAP, VWAP, sniper, and adaptive participation.
- **Adaptive Models** retraining on live data with governance checkpoints.
- **Performance Observer** logging KPIs, drawdowns, and compliance status in real time.

### Data Science Utilities
- **Feature Factory** for automated feature generation and selection.
- **Clustering & Regime Detection** to segment markets and tailor strategies.
- **Anomaly Detection** highlighting outliers in price, volume, and behavior.
- **Model Governance Suite** documenting lineage, validation, and approvals.
